Our Mission

“Pathfinders is a non-profit organization, dedicated to maintaining residential treatment facilities which address the needs of individuals recovering from the disease of alcoholism, drug addiction, co-occurring disorders, and homelessness. We strive to assist our resident’s families in dealing with the effects of alcoholism and drug addiction, while providing services to the community in our effort to reduce alcohol and drug related problems.”
